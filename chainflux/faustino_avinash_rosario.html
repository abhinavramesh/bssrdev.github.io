FAUSTINO AVINASH VEN ROSARIO

Cofounder & CEO at Mahendra NextWealth

Faustino AV Rosario is an entrepreneur with nearly 15 years of experience. He is currently the cofounder and CEO of Mahendra NextWealth, an ISO 9001:2008, ISO 27001 and PCI-DSS V3.2 certified IT and BPM company. He is an Engineer in Electronics and Communication from MIT, Manipal and holds Masters in Business Administration from OU, USA. His entrepreneurial journey more than a decade ago after a stint with renowned MNC’s in the area of IT-BPM operations management and consulting across verticals and geographies. He is one among the pioneers to explore Tier-2/3 town possibilities in India and build great success stories creating over 3500 Jobs and counting. He has varied interest across IT, BPM, Sports, Analytics, Event and Education. He is also a cofounder of Chainflux, Mahima Technology, Kickstartfc, Impetus Sports to name a few with some successful exits. He is a Lean and Six Sigma BlackBelt professional certified by ASQ and MBTI by CPPAP, Leaders Window by Accenture to name a few. He is also an investor, avid sports lover, and a founding member of kickstart foundation.
